About Me

Hi, my name is Arnab Sarkar. I'm a B.Tech student with a passion for computers that dates back to my childhood. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by the world of technology, from the inner workings of hardware to the magic of coding. My curiosity has always driven me to explore and learn more about how computers work and how they shape the world around us.

As I continue my academic journey in engineering, I’m constantly inspired by the endless possibilities technology offers. Whether it's building software, understanding complex algorithms, or exploring the latest trends in the tech world, I love diving into new challenges and expanding my knowledge.

Through my studies, I've developed strong problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of the tech industry, and I’m excited to continue this path toward becoming a skilled professional in the field.

I believe in the power of technology to change lives, and I am eager to contribute to that change through my work and passion for innovation.

My Story

Educational Background

I completed my Higher Secondary in 2024 from Sodepur High School, where I explored a wide array of subjects that helped shape my academic foundation. I studied core subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Statistics, which strengthened my problem-solving skills and ignited my passion for technology. Additionally, my focus on English and Bengali improved my communication abilities and appreciation for languages.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science at the University of Engineering & Management, Kolkata (UEMK), where I am in my 1st year. This course is providing me with a deeper understanding of computer science principles, programming, and technology-driven solutions. I’m excited to continue growing in this field and to further develop the skills needed to make a meaningful impact in the tech world.
